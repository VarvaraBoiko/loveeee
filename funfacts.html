Love Celebration: Valentine's Day is a day to celebrate love, kindness, and friendship. Heart Symbols: Everywhere you look, you'll see hearts—on cards, decorations, and treats. Card Exchange: Kids often exchange cute and colorful Valentine's Day cards with friends and classmates. Chocolate Treats: Many people enjoy yummy chocolates on this special day, shaped like hearts and other fun things. Cupid's Bow: Cupid, a tiny angel with a bow and arrow, is said to make people fall in love. Love Birds: Some people believe that on Valentine's Day, birds choose their special friends. Crafty Fun: Kids often make crafts like heart-shaped cards or drawings to share with those they care about. Friendship Focus: In some places, Valentine's Day is also about celebrating friendships and being kind to everyone. Sweet Surprises: Families might share sweet treats or do something special to show they care about each other. Fun Traditions: Different countries have unique ways of celebrating, making it a day full of fun traditions! Remember, Valentine's Day is not just for romantic love—it's also about appreciating friends and family!
